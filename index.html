The Experience

What to Expect

01
The Kitchen

Chef Christopher George's menu updates daily with the freshest possible ingredients — pristine sashimi and nigiri alongside contemporary dishes that honor traditional Japanese technique.

02
Binchotan Fire

Japanese white charcoal at 1000°C — a cooking method that caramelizes without contaminating, producing a smoke-free, intensely clean flavor impossible to replicate any other way.

03
The Beverage Program

Rare Japanese whiskies, hand-selected sake, creative cocktails, and thoughtfully composed zero-proof pairings — each chosen to balance and enhance the meal's natural progression.

04
Shared Dining

Many plates arrive family-style — designed not just to feed but to invite conversation. tomu san is built for lingering, for sharing, and for the kind of meal that gets talked about afterward.

05
The Setting

Minimalist design meets natural warmth. Soft light, open space, and a serene atmosphere that feels both cinematic and intimate — a dining room designed to disappear around the meal.

06
DC Ranch Location

20825 N Pima Rd, Suite 100 in DC Ranch Village — Scottsdale's most refined neighborhood. Easily accessible from Paradise Valley, North Scottsdale, and Carefree.
